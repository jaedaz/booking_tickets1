Frequently Asked Questions

  • Are there any discounts available?
    Yes, we offer discounts regularly. Please check our website for the latest deals.
  • How can I create an account?
    To create an account, click on the "Sign Up" button at the top of the page and enter your details.
  • How can I buy a ticket?
    Select the movie you want to watch, click on "Buy Ticket," and follow the instructions to complete your purchase.
  • Can I cancel my ticket?
    Yes, tickets can be canceled up to 24 hours before the showtime. Visit your account and go to "My Tickets" to cancel.
  • Do I need to print my ticket?
    No, you can show your e-ticket at the entrance to be scanned.
  • What payment methods are accepted?
    We accept credit/debit cards, PayPal, and other popular payment methods.
  • How do I reset my password?
    To reset your password, go to the "Forgot Password" page and follow the instructions.
  • Can I change my booking date?
    Yes, you can change your booking date by visiting "My Bookings" and selecting "Change Date".
  • Do you offer group discounts?
    Yes, group discounts are available for bookings of 10 or more tickets. Please contact our support team for details.
